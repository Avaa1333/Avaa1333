Milestones to Meet

  1. Successfully complete high school and get accepted to college.
    • Study hard in all my classes
    • Do well on final exams and standardized tests
    • Ask my counselor for help choosing and applying to the best colleges for me
  2. Study a related field, such as Biology or Kinesiology in college.
  3. Earn a Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree from a program accredited by the Commission on Accreditation in Physical Therapy Education
